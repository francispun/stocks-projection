Projects robotaxi market using 2025 ride-hailing size (M₀) for top 4 countries + others. Adjust sliders for market size, growth, and adoption. Uses Gompertz curve for penetration (slow start, rising acceleration, peak, then slowdown). Includes Tesla share, margin (32%), P/E (18x), price discount (30%), and elasticity boost (1.0) for demand growth. Adoption Readiness (r) scales speed, not cap. Shows Total Market, Robotaxi Market, Tesla Robotaxi, Penetration %, and Tesla market cap.
